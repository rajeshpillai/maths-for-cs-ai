# SAT Math Fundamentals

> Covers all specified SAT topics such as basic and advanced algebra, 2D and 3D geometry, trigonometry, functions, statistics, probability, and problem-solving.

**Prerequisites:** Algebra II, Integrated Math II, or Integrated Math II (Honors)

## Learning Outcomes

Upon completing SAT Math Fundamentals, students will be able to:

- Perform arithmetic operations with rational numbers, exponents, and radicals
- Solve linear equations and inequalities in one and two variables
- Work with systems of equations using substitution and elimination
- Apply geometric concepts: angles, triangles, polygons, circles, and solid geometry
- Use congruence and similarity criteria for problem solving
- Calculate length, area, and volume for plane figures and solids
- Apply trigonometric ratios and the unit circle
- Understand and analyze functions including transformations
- Solve quadratic and polynomial equations using multiple methods
- Work with exponential, rational, and radical expressions and equations
- Apply ratio, proportion, and percentage concepts to real-world problems
- Analyze data using statistics, probability, and correlation techniques
- Demonstrate fluency across all SAT-specified math topics
