# Integrated Math III

> Dive deep into the algebra of polynomials, practice graphing trigonometric functions, and master the algebra of radical functions and logarithms. Learn core concepts in combinatorics, probability, and statistics.

**Prerequisites:** Integrated Math II

## Learning Outcomes

Upon completing Integrated Math III, students will be able to:

- Factor and solve higher-order polynomial equations and graph polynomial functions
- Apply polynomial theorems (Factor, Remainder, Rational Roots)
- Work with rational expressions and graph reciprocal functions
- Graph and analyze radical functions and their transformations
- Solve exponential and logarithmic equations and model with exponential functions
- Work with real and complex number systems
- Analyze arithmetic and geometric sequences
- Write equations of circles and parabolas and work with their properties
- Apply the Law of Sines and Law of Cosines
- Graph and transform all six trigonometric functions
- Apply conditional probability, combinatorics, and basic statistical analysis
