## Learning Outcomes

Upon completing Integrated Math I, students will be able to:

- Solve linear equations, inequalities, and nonlinear equations
- Graph and model with two-variable linear equations and inequalities
- Solve systems of equations using elimination
- Work with unit conversions and direct/inverse variation
- Simplify rational and radical expressions and solve rational equations
- Understand function concepts including domain, range, and piecewise functions
- Solve absolute value equations and inequalities and graph absolute value functions
- Apply rules of exponents and model with exponential functions
- Perform polynomial operations and factor polynomials
- Apply geometric transformations in the coordinate plane
- Work with triangles, circles, and coordinate geometry
