# Geometry

> Learn how to compute length, area, and volume for a wide variety of objects. Discover relationships between angles and side lengths in right triangles.

**Prerequisites:** Algebra I

## Learning Outcomes

Upon completing Geometry, students will be able to:

- Construct and evaluate geometric proofs involving angles, lines, and triangles
- Apply geometric transformations including translations, rotations, reflections, and dilations
- Use congruence criteria (ASA, AAS, SAS, SSS, HL) to prove triangle congruence
- Apply similarity criteria and theorems to solve problems with similar figures
- Calculate arc lengths, sector areas, and apply circle theorems
- Work with circles in the coordinate plane including equations and tangent lines
- Apply the Pythagorean theorem and special right triangle relationships
- Use trigonometric ratios to solve right triangle problems
- Calculate surface areas and volumes of three-dimensional solids
- Apply probability concepts including conditional probability and combinatorics
