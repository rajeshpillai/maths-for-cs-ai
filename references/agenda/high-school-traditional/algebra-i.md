## Learning Outcomes

Upon completing Algebra I, students will be able to:

- Solve linear equations and inequalities, including compound and multi-variable cases
- Graph and analyze linear equations in multiple forms (slope-intercept, point-slope, standard)
- Solve systems of linear equations using elimination and graphical methods
- Work with units, direct variation, and inverse variation
- Simplify and operate with radical and rational expressions
- Understand and apply function concepts including domain, range, composition, and inverses
- Solve absolute value equations and inequalities and graph absolute value functions
- Apply rules of exponents and model with exponential functions
- Perform polynomial operations and factor polynomials
- Solve quadratic equations using multiple methods (factoring, completing the square, quadratic formula)
- Graph and analyze quadratic functions and model real-world scenarios
- Work with arithmetic and geometric sequences
- Analyze bivariate data using scatter plots and trend lines
