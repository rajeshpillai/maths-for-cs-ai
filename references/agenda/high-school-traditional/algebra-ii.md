## Learning Outcomes

Upon completing Algebra II, students will be able to:

- Work with real and complex number systems
- Factor, divide, and solve higher-order polynomial equations
- Apply polynomial theorems (Factor, Remainder, Rational Roots)
- Graph and analyze polynomial functions including end behavior
- Work with inverse functions and graph transformations
- Solve exponential and logarithmic equations using laws of logarithms
- Graph and analyze exponential and logarithmic functions
- Simplify, add, subtract, multiply, and divide rational expressions
- Graph radical and reciprocal functions and their transformations
- Understand parabolas as conic sections with focus-directrix properties
- Extend trigonometric ratios using the unit circle
- Graph and transform all six trigonometric functions
- Model real-world scenarios using trigonometric functions
